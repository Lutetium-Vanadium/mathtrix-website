Desolate Planet

Crash landing on this alien planet wasn't the plan. Having to fight for survival shouldn't have happened either. But here you are in a gruelling battle of endurance and wit to gain as many items from that shipwreck as possible to begin life anew on this planet. How far would you go to be the last one standing? Let's find out.

Rules

  • Each team must have three members. One member well-versed in twelfth grade math must be present.
  • Event has four rounds
  • The preliminary round consists of 15 questions - a mix of maths and bio.
  • The second round consists of identifying images and converting them into the elements of a determinant using given code, whose solution gives you a password to a Google form containing 5 questions to answer.
  • The third round is a rapid fire quiz.
  • The final round is a bidding round where all points and bonuses collected in the previous rounds will be used to buy items that will help grow participants plant or sabotage others' plants.
