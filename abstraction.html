Abstraction

Drawing inspiration from the worlds of logic and creativity, Abstraction seeks to explore the realm of mathematical art. Artistic skill and problem-solving abilities are prime characteristics to help participants in this innovative event. Artists are welcome to test their creativity and solve problems to create a masterpiece!

Rules

  • Each team must consist of two participants - one artist and one question solver (preferably aware of 12th math).
  • This event has only one round.
  • The question solver unlocks rgb codes of colours by answering.
  • The artist must make an abstract piece depicting given math topics while using colours unlocked by the question solver only.
  • The final piece to be submitted along with a 50 word write up explaining the art piece. Questions asked in relation to rgb colours also have to be answered.
