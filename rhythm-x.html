Rhythm-X

Combining the eccentricities of math and music, Rhythm-X puts creativity first. Blending lyrical and logical worlds, this event is sure to entertain.

Come as a team to divide and conquer,
Rhythm-X is the event for your musical spur!
Express yourself through your beats,
This event is sure to raise the roof and fill the streets!

Rules

  • Each team should comprise 2-5 members with at least one singer.
  • Team members must have knowledge of 11th grade math.
  • This event requires submissions prior to 20 November.
  • In the first round, participants will be given a set of questions to get certain digits used to get a code. This code is essential to unlocking the musical elements you can use.
  • The event heads will have a meeting with all participants on 17 November 2020 to explain the procedure to be followed for the first round.
  • The first round is an elimination round. Participants will be required to submit their songs by noon (12pm), 19 November 2020.
  • The second round will be a live quiz (to test the music and math knowledge of participants).
  • The song created by the participants should be 1-1.5 minutes.
  • All participants must adhere to the theme allotted.
  • Recordings of the song-making procedure must be shown.
