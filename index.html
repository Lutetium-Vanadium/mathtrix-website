The beloved math-centric fest organised by National Public School, Indiranagar, Mathtrix has seen students of various schools come together to compete over the years, putting on display the different skills they possess.

Completely student run, this fest covers the diverse spectrum of math, from its apex form as complex algebra, its applications entwined with biology and computer science, to its beauty manifested in music and art.
